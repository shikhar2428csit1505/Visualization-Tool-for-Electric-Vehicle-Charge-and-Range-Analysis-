// What We Analyze

Six Dimensions of
EV Intelligence

🔋
Charge Cycle Profiling

Track full and partial charge cycles across vehicle models. Identify optimal charging windows and understand how charging behavior affects long-term battery health and cell capacity retention.

📍
Range Estimation & Reality

Compare manufacturer-rated range against observed real-world figures across varying conditions — temperature, speed, terrain, and payload — to understand true vehicle performance envelopes.

⚡
Charging Speed Analytics

Visualize charge rate curves from Level 1, Level 2, and DC Fast Charging sessions. Discover how battery state-of-charge affects peak charging throughput and time-to-full metrics.

🌡️
Thermal Impact Modeling

Quantify how ambient temperature drives energy consumption and range loss. Cold and hot weather scenarios are plotted against baseline efficiency to surface hidden performance costs.

🚗
Fleet Efficiency Benchmarking

Benchmark individual vehicles against fleet-wide averages. Pinpoint outliers, predict maintenance windows, and optimize deployment strategies using data-driven fleet intelligence.

💡
Energy Cost Intelligence

Map energy consumption to real electricity pricing across geographies and time-of-use windows. Surface opportunities to reduce charging costs through behavioral and scheduling insights.
